About Us



"Lanying" one-stop service platform

Zhanjiang Lanying science agriculture Ltd, located in Zhanjiang, Guangdong, China, is a professional production of lucky bamboo and money tree with import and export right of private enterprises. With more than 20 years of lucky bamboo production and processing history, lanyin has rich experience in production ,technology and equipment strength and owns a large bamboo plantation.

Our products are sold throughout the country and the Unit States, Netherlands, Germany, Japan and other more than 30 countries and regions. Due to our professional technology of cultivating and design, our first class equipment and price, we have won a good reputation in lucky bamboo industry and have established a long-term good partnership with our customer in the world.

We have always pursued keep improving good service. We sincerely hope that we can build up a long-time and good business relation with you on mutual benefit.
